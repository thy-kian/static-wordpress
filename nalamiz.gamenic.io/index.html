About Us

Gamenic Nalamiz Tech is a Nepal based IT company providing web design, system development, web advertisement, and video production located in Liwali-8, Bhaktapur.

The office in Bhaktapur was opened in December 2019.

We are now an affiliated company of companies operating in 7 countries around the world, mainly in Asia. Our main clients are currently from Japan.

Company’s next step is to expand our markets into the US and Europe as well. We are also conducting business in education and trying to encourage people who are interested in working anywhere in the world using their IT and language skills set.

Vision

Our vision is to develop and grow as a significant IT service provider in order to become a market leader in offering high-quality Web and Software Development solutions in the competitive worldwide marketplace. Our professional, flexible and integrated process reflects in what we do. We always assist our customers in achieving their goals. We have the capacity to accelerate and quickly share your organization’s or business’s exceptional work or products.

Mission

Our mission is to enhancing the business growth of our customers with creative Design and Development to deliver market-defining high-quality solutions that provide value and reliable competitive advantage for our clients all over the world. We are continually looking for new ways to improve the quality of our products. Customer satisfaction is given top priority. We treat our customers with respect, which helps us retain existing customers and expand customer circle.
